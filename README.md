# Privacy Policy — cmmnspace POS

**Last updated: June 24, 2026**

## Overview

cmmnspace POS ("the App") is a point-of-sale application developed for internal use by cmmnspace retail staff. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects information.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
- Staff email address and authentication credentials used to log in to the App.

**Customer Information**
- Customer name, email address, and phone number when added to a transaction.
- Purchase history associated with customer accounts.

**Device Information**
- Device push notification token (used to send order alerts to staff).
- Biometric authentication data (Face ID) is processed entirely on-device by iOS and is never transmitted to our servers.

**Transaction Data**
- Orders, payments, discounts, and returns processed through the App.

## How We Use Information

- To authenticate staff and authorize access to the POS system.
- To process sales transactions and manage inventory.
- To send push notifications to staff for order updates.
- To generate sales reports for business operations.

## Data Storage

All data is stored on our servers at api.cmmn.space. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties except as required by law.

Authentication credentials are stored securely on-device using iOS Secure Enclave (SecureStore). Biometric data never leaves your device.

## Data Retention

Transaction and customer data is retained for as long as required for business operations and applicable tax/legal obligations.

## Security

We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Access to the system is protected by staff credentials and optional biometric authentication.
